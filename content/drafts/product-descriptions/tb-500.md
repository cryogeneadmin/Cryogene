TB-500, also referred to as Thymosin Beta-4 Fragment, is a synthetic analogue of a highly conserved 43-amino-acid protein present in virtually all nucleated cells. TB-500 corresponds to the actin-binding domain of Thymosin Beta-4. CAS number: 77591-33-4. Molecular formula: C₂₁₂H₃₅₀N₅₆O₇₈S. Molecular weight: 4963.5 g/mol.

## Research context

[DRAFT — REVIEW AND ADAPT] Thymosin Beta-4 and its fragments have been investigated in preclinical research models for their roles in actin sequestration, cell migration, and tissue response following injury.
