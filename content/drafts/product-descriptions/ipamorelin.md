Ipamorelin is a synthetic pentapeptide growth hormone secretagogue. It acts as a selective agonist at the ghrelin/growth hormone secretagogue receptor (GHS-R1a). Its sequence is Aib-His-D-2-Nal-D-Phe-Lys-NH₂. CAS number: 170851-70-4. Molecular formula: C₃₈H₄₉N₉O₅. Molecular weight: 711.86 g/mol.

## Research context

[DRAFT — REVIEW AND ADAPT] Ipamorelin has been investigated in preclinical research models for its selective stimulation of growth hormone release from the pituitary gland. Unlike other growth hormone secretagogues, research models suggest it does not significantly stimulate cortisol, aldosterone, or prolactin release at pharmacologically relevant concentrations. It appears in published research examining growth hormone axis regulation and GHS-R1a pharmacology.
