Thymosin Alpha-1 (Tα1) is a 28-amino-acid acetylated peptide originally isolated from thymosin Fraction 5, a calf thymus extract. Its sequence is Ac-Ser-Asp-Ala-Ala-Val-Asp-Thr-Ser-Ser-Glu-Ile-Thr-Thr-Lys-Asp-Leu-Lys-Glu-Lys-Lys-Glu-Val-Val-Glu-Glu-Ala-Glu-Asn-OH. CAS number: 62304-98-7. Molecular formula: C₁₂₉H₂₁₅N₃₃O₅₅. Molecular weight: 3108.3 g/mol.

## Research context

[DRAFT — REVIEW AND ADAPT] Thymosin Alpha-1 has been investigated in preclinical and clinical research for its modulatory effects on immune cell activity, including T-lymphocyte proliferation and dendritic cell maturation. It appears in published research on thymic peptides, innate immune response, and toll-like receptor signalling pathways.
