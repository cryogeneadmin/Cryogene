**[DRAFT — REVIEW AND ADAPT]**

Tirzepatide is a synthetic 39-amino-acid dual agonist peptide that activates both the glucose-dependent insulinotropic polypeptide (GIP) receptor and the glucagon-like peptide-1 (GLP-1) receptor. It incorporates a C₂₀ fatty diacid attached via a gamma-glutamic acid linker to facilitate albumin binding and extend half-life in research models. CAS number: 2023788-19-2. Molecular formula: C₂₂₅H₃₄₈N₄₈O₆₈. Molecular weight: 4813.5 g/mol.

## Research context

[DRAFT — REVIEW AND ADAPT] Tirzepatide has been investigated in preclinical and clinical research models for its effects on GIP and GLP-1 receptor co-activation, with research examining insulin secretion, glucagon regulation, and metabolic signalling pathways. It appears in peer-reviewed literature on incretin biology and dual receptor pharmacology.
