GHK-Cu (Copper Tripeptide-1) is a naturally occurring copper complex of the tripeptide glycyl-L-histidyl-L-lysine. It is found in human plasma, saliva, and urine. CAS number: 89030-95-5. Molecular formula: C₁₄H₂₄CuN₆O₄. Molecular weight: 403.91 g/mol.

## Research context

[DRAFT — REVIEW AND ADAPT] GHK-Cu has been investigated in laboratory research for its interactions with copper transport and its role in modulating gene expression in skin fibroblasts and other cell types. Research models have examined its effects on collagen synthesis, antioxidant enzyme activity, and connective tissue remodelling processes. It appears in published research on copper-binding peptides and skin biology.
