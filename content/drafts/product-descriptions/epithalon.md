Epithalon (also spelled Epitalon) is a synthetic tetrapeptide with the sequence Ala-Glu-Asp-Gly. It is a synthetic analogue of Epithalamin, a polypeptide extract from the bovine pineal gland. CAS number: 307297-39-8. Molecular formula: C₁₄H₂₂N₄O₉. Molecular weight: 390.35 g/mol.

## Research context

[DRAFT — REVIEW AND ADAPT] Epithalon has been investigated in preclinical research models for its interactions with telomerase activity and telomere biology. Research models — primarily in vitro and in rodent studies — have examined its effects on telomere length in somatic cells and on pineal gland function. It appears in published research on peptide bioregulators and telomere biology, predominantly in Eastern European literature.
