Melanotan II (MT-II) is a synthetic cyclic heptapeptide analogue of alpha-melanocyte-stimulating hormone (α-MSH). It is a non-selective melanocortin receptor agonist. CAS number: 121062-08-6. Molecular formula: C₅₀H₆₉N₁₅O₉. Molecular weight: 1024.18 g/mol.

## Research context

[DRAFT — REVIEW AND ADAPT] Melanotan II has been investigated in preclinical research models for its interactions with melanocortin receptors (MC1R, MC3R, MC4R, MC5R). Research models have examined its effects on pigmentation pathways, central nervous system melanocortin receptor activation, and food intake regulation in animal models. It appears in published research on melanocortin receptor pharmacology and pigmentation biology.
