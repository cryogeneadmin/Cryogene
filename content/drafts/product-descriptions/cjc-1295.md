**[DRAFT — REVIEW AND ADAPT]**

CJC-1295, also known as DAC:GRF (Drug Affinity Complex: Growth Hormone Releasing Factor), is a synthetic 30-amino-acid analogue of growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH). It incorporates a maleimidoproprionic acid (MPA) modification at the lysine residue at position 29 that enables covalent binding to endogenous albumin, extending its half-life in research models. CAS number: 863288-34-0. Molecular formula: C₁₅₂H₂₅₂N₄₄O₄₂S. Molecular weight: 3647.28 g/mol.

## Research context

[DRAFT — REVIEW AND ADAPT] CJC-1295 has been investigated in preclinical and clinical research models for its effects on growth hormone-releasing hormone receptor (GHRHR) activation and the pharmacokinetics of sustained GHRH analogue action following albumin binding. Research has examined its role in growth hormone release kinetics and pulsatile GH secretion patterns. It appears in published literature on GHRH analogues and the growth hormone axis.
