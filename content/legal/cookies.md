## What are cookies?

[DRAFT — PENDING SOLICITOR REVIEW] Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your device when you visit. They allow the website to remember information about your visit — such as whether you have accepted our terms, verified your age, or added items to your basket. Cookies are not programs and cannot carry viruses or access your device.

These cookies are essential for the website to function. They cannot be disabled.
