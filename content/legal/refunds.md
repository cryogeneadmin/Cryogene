# Refund and Returns Policy

*Last updated: 13 April 2026*

---

## Your right to cancel (unopened orders)

[DRAFT — PENDING SOLICITOR REVIEW] If you are a consumer, you have the right to cancel your order within fourteen (14) calendar days of receiving the goods, without giving a reason, under the Consumer Contracts (Information, Cancellation and Additional Charges) Regulations 2013. To exercise your right to cancel, you must notify us in writing within the fourteen-day period via our [contact form](/contact) or by email to [TO BE CONFIRMED BY SAM — business email].

The right to cancel applies only to **unopened** products. Research chemicals that have been opened cannot be returned for safety, chain-of-custody, and contamination-prevention reasons.

## No returns on opened products

[DRAFT — PENDING SOLICITOR REVIEW] Due to the nature of research chemicals, we cannot accept returns of any product that has been opened. Once the seal on a vial has been broken, we cannot verify the integrity, purity, or chain of custody of the contents, and we cannot re-list or dispose of returned opened products safely. This policy does not affect your statutory rights regarding faulty goods.

## Damaged in transit

[DRAFT — PENDING SOLICITOR REVIEW] If your order arrives damaged, please:

1. Do not discard the original packaging, including the outer box, bubble wrap, and any ice packs
2. Take photographs of the damaged outer packaging and the damaged product before handling it further
3. Contact us within **48 hours** of delivery via our [contact form](/contact), including your order number and photographs
4. We will investigate and respond within one working day

If damage is confirmed, we will arrange a replacement or refund at our discretion, in accordance with your statutory rights.

## Incorrect item received

[DRAFT — PENDING SOLICITOR REVIEW] If you receive a product that does not match your order, please contact us within **48 hours** of delivery via our [contact form](/contact) with your order number and a description of the discrepancy. We will arrange collection of the incorrect item and dispatch the correct product at no additional cost, or issue a full refund if the correct product is unavailable.

## Refund process

[DRAFT — PENDING SOLICITOR REVIEW] Where a refund is approved, we will process it to your original payment method within fourteen (14) days of:

- Receiving the returned goods (for cancelled orders); or
- Confirming the damage or discrepancy (for damaged/incorrect items)

We will notify you by email when the refund has been processed. Bank processing times may mean the funds take an additional 3–5 working days to appear in your account, depending on your payment provider.

## Return shipping

[DRAFT — PENDING SOLICITOR REVIEW] If you are returning a product under your cancellation right (unopened products only), the cost of return shipping is your responsibility. We recommend using a tracked service and retaining proof of postage. We are not responsible for returned items that are lost in transit.

If we have confirmed that you received a faulty or incorrect item, we will cover the cost of return shipping.
