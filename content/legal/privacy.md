## 4. Data retention

[DRAFT — PENDING SOLICITOR REVIEW] We retain personal data for the following periods:

- **Order records** (name, address, order details): 6 years after the date of purchase, for HMRC record-keeping purposes
- **Customer accounts:** 2 years after the date of last login, after which the account and associated data are deleted
- **Analytics data:** 26 months (Vercel Analytics default)
- **Age verification records:** Not retained — the age gate confirmation is recorded as a timestamp against the order, not as identity data

[DRAFT — PENDING SOLICITOR REVIEW] Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

- **Portability** of your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the [contact form](/contact). We will respond within one calendar month.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at [ico.org.uk](https://ico.org.uk).
